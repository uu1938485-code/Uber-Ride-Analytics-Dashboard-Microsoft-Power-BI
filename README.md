I built a comprehensive 5-page interactive Uber Analytics Dashboard analyzing ride bookings, revenue performance, customer behavior, vehicle types, and driver ratings — covering 149K completed rides and $52M in total revenue!

**Key Dashboard Highlights**

 **KPI Overview**
149K Completed Rides | 57K Lost Bookings | $52M Revenue | 2.51M km Distance

**Revenue & Payment Analysis**
UPI is the top payment method ($23M), followed by Cash and Wallet

**Customer Behavior Analysis**
83K first-time riders dominate the platform

  **Vehicle Type Performance**
Auto is the most popular and highest revenue-generating vehicle

**Monthly Revenue Trends**
Peak in March; consistent performance across most months

**Ratings & Performance**
Customer Rating: 4.40 | Driver Rating: 4.23

**Key Insights**

Strong ride completion but 57K lost bookings indicate improvement area

UPI dominates as the preferred payment method

High number of new customers (83K)

Customer satisfaction is strong (4.40 rating)

Revenue remains stable with seasonal peaks
